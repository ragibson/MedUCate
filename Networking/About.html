About

Meducate is a fun and upcoming trivia game designed to help kids learn about
various health topics. The questions come in different sets, to allow for general
health education while opening the possibility to create questions targeted to
the individual child's health concerns.


Studies have shown that presenting new information to children in the form of games
helps them to learn that information more easily and fosters an environment in which
they WANT to learn the information. We hope our game will help to reach this
audience and make it easier for parents and doctors to get their kids and patients
interested in and prepared to live a healtier life.
